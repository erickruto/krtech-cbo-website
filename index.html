About Us

Krtech CBO is a community-based organization committed to transforming livelihoods through sustainable livestock management. We believe livestock is more than income—it is food security, culture, and resilience. We empower communities through training, innovation, and collaboration.

Our Mission

To improve community well-being by promoting sustainable livestock management practices that enhance productivity, health, and stability.

Our Vision

A thriving community where livestock farming is productive, sustainable, and a reliable source of income and food security.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – Transparent and accountable in all our work.
  • Community Empowerment – Building skills and capacities at the grassroots.
  • Sustainability – Protecting animals, people, and the environment.
  • Partnership – Collaborating with communities, governments, and stakeholders.

Our Programs

Livestock Health & Veterinary Support

We provide veterinary services, vaccination campaigns, and disease prevention training to ensure healthier, stronger animals.

Breeding & Productivity

We support modern breeding, feeding, and husbandry practices to improve livestock quality and output.

Farmer Training

We equip farmers with practical skills through workshops and training on sustainable livestock management.

Market Access & Value Addition

We help farmers access better markets and value-add activities like milk processing, hides, and meat production.

Community Empowerment

We champion environmental conservation, food security, and resilience through education and empowerment programs.
